R Training for Beginners and Intermediate Users

Welcome to the HARP Team R Training Portal

This training resource has been created by Daryn Sutton to help team members develop their R programming skills. Whether you're just getting started with R or looking to expand your knowledge, you'll find structured learning materials organized by topic.

Each module includes:

  • Detailed explanations with examples
  • Code snippets with thorough comments
  • Interactive exercises to test your understanding
  • Links to further resources
